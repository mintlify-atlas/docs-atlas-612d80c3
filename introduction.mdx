## What is TruequeU?

TruequeU is a campus marketplace built for students, by students. It connects university community members looking to buy, sell, or trade items — from textbooks and electronics to services and more — all within a trusted student network.

Whether you need a copy of Calculus Stewart for next semester or want to sell your old laptop before summer break, TruequeU makes it easy to find what you need and reach the people around you.

## Who is TruequeU for?

TruequeU is designed for **university students** who want to:

- **Sell** items they no longer need — textbooks from last semester, electronics, furniture
- **Buy** from fellow students at fair prices without going through large commercial platforms
- **Trade** items within a trusted community where everyone shares the same campus

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your name, email, and a secure password. Your account ties your listings and chats together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse listings">
    Explore everything your campus community has to offer. Use the search bar to find specific items or filter by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact a seller">
    Found something you want? Open a chat with the seller directly from the listing detail page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post your own items">
    Create a listing for anything you want to sell or trade. Add a photo, set a price, and publish in seconds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tech stack

TruequeU is a client-side React application built with modern tooling:

- **React 19** — UI library
- **TypeScript** — type safety across the entire codebase
- **React Router v7** — client-side routing
- **Zustand** — lightweight global state management with localStorage persistence
- **Tailwind CSS v4** — utility-first styling
- **Vite** — fast development and build tooling
