This guide covers everything related to your TruequeU account: signing up, understanding password requirements, logging in, and signing out.

## Sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
    Navigate to `/signup` or select **Create one now** from the login page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    The registration form has the following fields:

    | Field | Input name | Required | Notes |
    |---|---|---|---|
    | Full Name | `fullName` | Yes | Your display name shown to other students. |
    | University Email | `email` | Yes | Must be a valid email address. Each address can only be registered once. |
    | Major / Faculty | `major` | Yes | Your field of study (e.g. `Engineering`). |
    | Password | `password` | Yes | Must meet the password requirements below. |
    | Confirm password | `confirmPassword` | Yes | Must exactly match the value entered in the Password field. |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a strong password">
    TruequeU validates your password before creating your account. The `validatePassword` function enforces the following rules:

    - Minimum **8 characters**
    - At least **1 uppercase letter** (A–Z)
    - At least **1 lowercase letter** (a–z)
    - At least **1 number** (0–9)
    - At least **1 special character** — any character that is not a letter or number (e.g. `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`, `%`)

    <Warning>
      All five rules must be satisfied simultaneously. If any rule is not met, the form will display an error and your account will not be created. Double-check your password before submitting.
    </Warning>

    <Tip>
      A passphrase-style password such as `Campus42!Trade` is easier to remember and satisfies every requirement: it exceeds 8 characters, contains uppercase and lowercase letters, a number, and a special character.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Select **Create Account**.

    If the email address is already registered, the form will display an error and you will need to use a different address or sign in to your existing account.

    On success, you are logged in automatically and redirected to the home page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password requirements reference

The following table summarises every rule enforced by `validatePassword` in `auth.service.ts`:

| Rule | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Minimum length | 8 characters |
| Uppercase letter | At least 1 character matching `[A-Z]` |
| Lowercase letter | At least 1 character matching `[a-z]` |
| Number | At least 1 character matching `[0-9]` |
| Special character | At least 1 character that is not a letter or number (`[^a-zA-Z0-9]`) |

## Log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the login page">
    Navigate to `/login` or select **Sign In** in the navigation bar.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    The login form has two fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Email Address** | The university email you registered with. |
    | **Password** | Your account password. |

    Select **Sign In**.

    <Tip>
      You can also sign in with your **University Microsoft Account** using the button below the form. This is the fastest option if your institution uses Microsoft 365.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Successful login">
    On a successful login, you are redirected to the home page (`/`). TruequeU stores your session in the browser using `localStorage` under the key `marketplace-storage`, so you remain logged in across page refreshes.

    From the navigation bar you can access:
    - **Profile** (`/profile`) — view and manage your account details.
    - **Chat** (`/chat`) — read and reply to messages from buyers or sellers.
    - **Sell** (`/create`) — publish a new listing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If your email or password is incorrect, the login form will display: *"Email o contraseña incorrectos. Regístrate si no tienes cuenta."* Check your credentials and try again, or create a new account if you have not registered yet.
</Info>

## Sign out

To sign out, open your **Profile** page at `/profile` and select the sign-out option. This clears the `user` state from the store, ending your session. You will need to sign in again to access protected features such as creating listings or contacting sellers.

<Warning>
  Signing out does not delete your account or your listings. Your data is retained in `localStorage` and will be available the next time you sign in.
</Warning>
