TruequeU is a peer-to-peer marketplace for university students to trade and sell items on campus. This guide walks you through opening the app, creating an account, and publishing your first listing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Navigate to the TruequeU app in your browser. You will land on the home page, which displays featured listings from students on your campus.

    If you already have an account, select **Sign In** in the navigation bar to log in and skip to step 3.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create an account">
    Select **Create one now** on the login page, or navigate directly to `/signup`.

    Fill in the registration form with the following fields:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **Full Name** | Your real name as it will appear to other students. |
    | **University Email** | Your institutional email address (e.g. `user@uni.edu`). |
    | **Major / Faculty** | Your area of study (e.g. `Engineering`). |
    | **Password** | A strong password that meets the requirements below. |
    | **Confirm password** | Re-enter your password to verify it matches. |

    <Note>
      Your password must be at least 8 characters long and include all of the following:
      - 1 uppercase letter (A–Z)
      - 1 lowercase letter (a–z)
      - 1 number (0–9)
      - 1 special character (e.g. `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`)

      Example of a valid password: `Juan123!`
    </Note>

    Select **Create Account**. You will be logged in automatically and redirected to the home page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse existing listings">
    From the home page, select **Listings** in the navigation bar to view all available items.

    Use the search bar to filter listings by keyword. Each listing card shows the item title, price, category, and availability status (`available`, `reserved`, or `sold`).

    Select any listing card to open its detail page and see the full description, images, and seller information.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first listing">
    Select **Sell** or navigate to `/create` to open the listing creation form.

    Provide the following details for your item:

    | Field | Description |
    |---|---|
    | **title** | A short, descriptive name for the item. |
    | **description** | Details about condition, size, age, or anything relevant. |
    | **price** | The amount you want to receive (numeric). |
    | **category** | The category that best fits the item. |
    | **images** | One or more photos of the item. |

    New listings are published with a status of `available` and appear at the top of the listings feed immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Contact a seller">
    To reach out about a listing you are interested in, open the listing detail page and select **Contact seller**.

    This opens a private chat between you (the buyer) and the seller, linked to that specific listing. Type your message and press **Send**. The seller will be notified and can reply from their chat inbox at `/chat`.

    <Note>
      You must be logged in to contact a seller. If you are not logged in, you will be redirected to the login page.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
