---
title: "Create a listing"
description: "Post an item for sale or trade on TruequeU by filling out the listing form."
---

Creating a listing makes your item visible to the entire campus community. The form is available at `/create` and requires you to be logged in.

<Warning>
  You must be signed in to create a listing. If you visit `/create` without an active session, you will see a prompt to log in.
</Warning>

## Form walkthrough

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter an item title">
    Type a short, descriptive name for your item in the **Item Title** field.

    - Minimum: 3 characters
    - Maximum: 100 characters
    - Example: `Calculus Stewart 8th Edition`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set a price">
    Enter a numeric price in the **Price ($)** field.

    - Minimum: `$0` (free items are allowed)
    - Maximum: `$999,999`
    - Accepts decimals (step of `0.01`)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Select one of the four available categories from the **Category** dropdown:

    - **Electronics**
    - **Books**
    - **Services**
    - **Other**

    The default selection is **Electronics**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write a description">
    Describe the item's condition, your reasons for selling, and any other relevant details in the **Description** field.

    - Minimum: 10 characters
    - Maximum: 1,000 characters
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload an image">
    Click **Browse files** or drag and drop an image onto the upload area. The image is read as a base64 data URL and stored with the listing.

    If you skip this step, TruequeU uses a placeholder image (`https://via.placeholder.com/400`).

    To remove an uploaded image, click the red **✕** button in the top-right corner of the preview.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish the listing">
    Click **Publish Listing** to submit the form. TruequeU validates all fields before saving.

    On success, the new listing is added to the global store and you are redirected to the home page (`/`).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Validation rules

Validation is handled by `validateListing` in `src/services/listing.service.ts`. The rules are applied in order — the first failing rule stops submission and displays an error message.

| Field | Rule | Error condition |
|---|---|---|
| Title | At least 3 characters (after trimming whitespace) | Fewer than 3 characters |
| Title | No more than 100 characters | More than 100 characters |
| Description | At least 10 characters (after trimming whitespace) | Fewer than 10 characters |
| Description | No more than 1,000 characters | More than 1,000 characters |
| Price | Must be a valid number | Non-numeric input |
| Price | Cannot be negative | Value below `0` |
| Price | Cannot exceed $999,999 | Value above `999999` |

Errors appear as a highlighted message above the form. Changing any input clears the current error.

## What happens after publishing

When the form passes validation, TruequeU:

1. Generates a unique listing ID using `listing-${Date.now()}`
2. Sets the status to `available`
3. Sets `isFavorite` to `false`
4. Calls `addListing` on the Zustand store, prepending the new listing to the `listings` array
5. Navigates you to `/` (the home page)

<Note>
  New listings created through the form are stored in Zustand and persisted to `localStorage` under the key `marketplace-storage`. They appear alongside seeded items from `src/data/items.ts` on the listings page.
</Note>

## Navigating to the form

You can reach the create-listing page from:

- The **+ New Listing** button on your [profile page](/features/profile)
- Navigating directly to `/create`
