The profile page at `/profile` is your personal hub on TruequeU. It shows your account details, all the listings you have posted, and quick actions for managing your account.

<Warning>
  You must be signed in to view your profile. If you visit `/profile` without an active session, TruequeU shows a prompt with **Sign In** and **Sign Up** buttons.
</Warning>

## Profile header

At the top of the page you will see:

- **Avatar** — a unique avatar generated by the [DiceBear Avataaars API](https://www.dicebear.com/) using your user ID as the seed: `https://api.dicebear.com/7.x/avataaars/svg?seed={user.id}`
- **Name** — your display name
- **Email** — the email address associated with your account

## Actions

Two buttons appear next to your profile info:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="+ New Listing" icon="plus" href="/features/create-listing">
    Navigates to `/create` to open the listing creation form.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sign Out" icon="arrow-right-from-bracket">
    Calls the `logout` action on the Zustand store, setting `user` to `null` and effectively ending your session.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Your listings

Below your profile header, the page displays all listings where `ownerId` matches your user ID. This includes both seeded items (from `src/data/items.ts`) and any listings you have created through the form.

```ts
const myListings = allListings.filter((l) => l.ownerId === user.id);
```

Listings are rendered using the same `ListingList` grid component used on the main listings page, so each card has the same **Chat** and **Details** buttons.

### No listings yet

If you haven't posted anything, the page shows an empty state with a **Create your first listing** link that navigates to `/create`.

## Signing out

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Navigate to `/profile` from the navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Sign Out">
    Click the **Sign Out** button in the top-right area of the profile header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Session ended">
    Your session is cleared. You remain on the current page, but features that require authentication (creating listings, messaging, viewing profile) will prompt you to log in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Signing out only clears the `user` field in the Zustand store. Your listings and chat history remain saved in `localStorage` and will still be there if you sign back in.
</Note>
